Zhanxin Wu

I am a master student at National University of Singapore (NUS) advised by David Hsu. I work at the intersection of robotics and machine learning, with the goal of enabling robots to act intelligently in the real world.

Previously, I worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant with Prof. Zhao in the Lab of Energy Internet. After that, I was a Robotics Institute Summer Scholars (RISS) at Carnegie Mellon University Robotics Institute advised by Dr. Jean Oh. Currently, I am working on navigation with LLMs at the NUS Adacomp. I received my B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Chinese University of Hongkong, Shenzhen (CUHKSZ) in 2022.
